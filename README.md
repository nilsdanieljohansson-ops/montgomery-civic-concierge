Montgomery Civic Concierge

AI-powered civic assistant that helps Montgomery residents instantly find the right city service, understand what to do next, and generate ready-to-send reports using city data and public web intelligence.

🚧 Built for the GenAI.Works Hackathon



The Idea in 10 Seconds

City services are hard to navigate.

Montgomery Civic Concierge lets residents simply ask a question:

“How do I report a pothole?”

The AI instantly:

• routes the request to the correct department
• explains what to do next
• uses city data for context
• generates a ready-to-send report

Think of it as ChatGPT for city services.



Example Questions

Residents can ask natural questions like:

How do I report a pothole?

Where is the nearest tornado shelter?

How do I report a code violation?

Who do I contact about missed trash pickup?

The system instantly returns:

• the correct city department
• clear next steps
• relevant civic data
• a formatted report template

Why This Matters

City information is often fragmented across departments.

Residents don't know:

• where to report issues
• which department is responsible
• what steps to take

Montgomery Civic Concierge simplifies civic engagement and reduces friction between citizens and city services.
